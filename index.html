How to use this dashboardshow / hide

Everything runs in your browser — nothing is uploaded or stored anywhere. The board stays empty until you load your own files. Your files never leave your computer; the only external request is a one-time fetch of two open-source CSV/Excel parsers on your first upload.

  1. Drop your Daily report (the raw shipment CSV) and your Target file. Optionally add the Accounts (AM/KAM) directory file.
  2. The board fills in instantly — KPIs, per-account flags (CALL drops, went silent, overdue calls, behind pace, pickup-pending, RTO), AM & KAM scorecards, and a sortable account table.
  3. Use From / To to view a single day or any range. Hover any column heading or badge to see what it means. Click a KPI tile, an AM/KAM card, or use the filters to narrow the table. Copy summary gives a shareable text rundown.

Upload files in any order, and re-drop just the daily CSV each day — targets and accounts stay until you change them. With the Accounts directory loaded, every account in it is shown and accounts without a target are treated as target 0. KAM fills in from the directory's KAM column as you populate it. Business cycle runs the 26th → 25th.
